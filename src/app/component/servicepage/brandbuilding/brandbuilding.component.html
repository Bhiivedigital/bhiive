FAQs

1. Do we need brand strategy before visual design?

Yes. Brand strategy creates the foundation for all visual and messaging decisions, ensuring your branding communicates the right message.

2. How long does brand building take?

Brand strategy and identity typically take 3–5 weeks. Full brand development with messaging and collateral may take 6–8 weeks.

3. Do we need a full rebrand if we already have a logo?

Not always. Sometimes improving the messaging and positioning delivers better results than redesigning the entire visual identity.

4. Can you help with both branding and marketing?

Yes. We align brand building with SEO, paid ads, content marketing, and digital growth strategies for better business results.
