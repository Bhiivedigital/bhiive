The Problem With Most Paid Marketing Agencies

Most agencies optimize for metrics that look impressive in reports but don't grow your business. High click-through rates don't matter if the leads don't convert. Low CPCs don't matter if the traffic is unqualified.

We optimize for what actually matters: cost per qualified lead, conversion rate, customer acquisition cost, and return on ad spend (ROAS).

Paid Marketing in 2026: What's Changed

Google's AI-powered Smart Bidding and Meta's machine learning systems now reward advertisers who provide strong conversion data, high-quality creative, and optimized landing pages.

Today, creative quality, accurate tracking, landing page optimization, and first-party data are more important than manual targeting hacks.

FAQs

1. What's the minimum budget for Google or Meta Ads?

We typically recommend ₹30,000–₹50,000/month for Google Ads and ₹20,000/month for Meta Ads to gather enough optimization data.

2. How long before paid campaigns generate results?

Google Ads can generate leads within the first week, while Meta campaigns often require 2–4 weeks of testing and optimization.

3. Do you create ad creatives and copy?

Yes. We handle ad copywriting, campaign messaging, and creative strategy for paid campaigns.

4. Can you manage existing campaigns?

Absolutely. We audit and optimize existing Google Ads, Meta Ads, and LinkedIn campaigns for better performance.

5. Do you guarantee results?

We don't guarantee specific lead numbers, but we guarantee transparent management, ongoing optimization, and honest reporting.
