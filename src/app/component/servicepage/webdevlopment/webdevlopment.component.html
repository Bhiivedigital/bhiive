The standards for websites have changed dramatically. Google's Core Web Vitals impact rankings, mobile-first indexing is now the default, and website speed directly affects both SEO performance and conversions.

A modern business website needs speed, technical SEO, mobile-first UX, conversion-focused structure, strong security, and optimized content architecture.

FAQs

A standard business website usually takes 3–5 weeks. Larger custom projects may take 6–10 weeks depending on features and integrations.

2. Do you redesign existing websites?

Yes. We redesign and optimize existing websites to improve speed, SEO, UI/UX, and conversion performance.

3. Will my website be mobile-optimized?

Absolutely. Every website we build follows a mobile-first approach and is tested across devices before launch.

4. Can I update the website myself?

Yes. We provide CMS-based solutions and training so you can easily manage routine content updates.

5. Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes. We offer ongoing website maintenance, security updates, performance monitoring, and technical support plans.
